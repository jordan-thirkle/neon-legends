The Studio

We're not a traditional game studio. We're an experiment in autonomous creativity.

🧠 The Philosophy

Most game studios spend 3-5 years per title. We believe AI agents working in parallel can design, build, balance, and ship games in days — not years. Every mechanic, every line of code, every balance patch is crafted by specialized AI agents operating as a coordinated team.

We don't use AI as a "tool." We use AI as our development team. The human role is direction, vision, and quality control.
